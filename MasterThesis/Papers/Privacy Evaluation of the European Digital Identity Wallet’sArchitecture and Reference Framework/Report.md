# 2 Literature

### PRISMA
How authors filtered actual literature ? Using **PRISMA** (the following standardized flow diagram):

The flow diagram is a visual representation that tracks the number of records included in the paper. We have 4 phases:

- **Identification:** The initial number of records found through database searching and other sources (and the removal of duplicates).
    
- **Screening:** The number of unique records screened (usually by title and abstract) and the number excluded at this stage.
    
- **Eligibility:** The number of full-text articles assessed for eligibility and the specific reasons why certain full-text articles were excluded.
    
- **Included:** The final number of studies included in the qualitative synthesis and/or quantitative synthesis (meta-analysis).

By adhering to these guidelines, researchers minimize bias and ensure their systematic reviews are credible to the scientific communities.
They inspected a lot of literature concerning EUDIW and general privacy and security requirement on Identity wallets.
