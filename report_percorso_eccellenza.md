# Anonymous credentials
In this month, I had the chance to explore and navigate trough the wide literature of Anonymous credentials, with a particular sight to the new EUDI (EUropean Digital Identity) wallet application.
Under the guide of my professor supervisor Ivan Visconti, which pointed out for me a clear path in the constellation of the anonymous credentials literature, I foud my way trough tons of papers and I was actually able to create my own star.
## How we organised
I asked professor Visconti, which I had the chance to meet during the Advanced Cryptography course, what was his proposal, since I am actually really interested in cryptographic matter. He pointed out a few proposal, on topics on which he was working. Among them I found really fascinating the EUDI wallet study.
In particuar, the requirement was to find vulnerabilities in the actual EUDI wallet implementation.
I started by studying the scientific literature which builds around the world of credentials, anonymous credentials, device binding, post-quantum cryptographya and post-quantum anonymous credentials. The professor pointed out for me a lot of _key papers_ which clarified the state of the art and pointed out different potential vulnerabilities.
During this month, I frequently met the professor in order to share with him the knowledge that I acquired, the potential vulnerabilities and/or mitigations that I found and to report him the actual advance in state of the art.
